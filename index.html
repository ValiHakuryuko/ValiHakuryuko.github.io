About

I'm a university student on the path to becoming a professional pentester. Currently deep in a focused study sprint — working toward the Burp Suite Certified Practitioner (BSCP) and building the skills needed for OSCP.

This blog is where I document what I learn: CTF write-ups, technique breakdowns, lab walkthroughs, and anything else worth writing down. If you're learning too, pull up a chair.

When I'm not breaking web apps, I'm writing Python scripts, grinding TryHackMe rooms, and making my way through Hack The Box challenges.
